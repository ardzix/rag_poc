DEFINISI KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPI)
PT ARNATECH INDONESIA

==============================================

1. NET PROMOTER SCORE (NPS)

Definisi:
NPS adalah metrik untuk mengukur kepuasan dan loyalitas pelanggan berdasarkan kemungkinan mereka merekomendasikan produk/layanan ke orang lain.

Cara Pengukuran:
- Survey dengan pertanyaan: "Seberapa besar kemungkinan Anda merekomendasikan layanan kami ke teman/kolega?" (skala 0-10)
- Kategorisasi:
  * Promoters (9-10): Pelanggan sangat puas dan loyal
  * Passives (7-8): Pelanggan puas tapi tidak antusias
  * Detractors (0-6): Pelanggan tidak puas

Formula:
NPS = % Promoters - % Detractors

Range Nilai:
-100 hingga +100

Interpretasi:
- NPS > 70: Excellent
- NPS 50-70: Great
- NPS 30-50: Good
- NPS 0-30: Needs Improvement
- NPS < 0: Critical

Benchmark Industry:
- Technology/SaaS: 30-40
- E-commerce: 50-60
- Financial Services: 30-35

Target Perusahaan:
- Q3 2025: 75-83 (tergantung regional)
- Q4 2025: 85 (semua regional)

==============================================

2. CUSTOMER SATISFACTION INDEX (CSI)

Definisi:
CSI mengukur tingkat kepuasan pelanggan secara keseluruhan terhadap produk dan layanan yang diberikan.

Cara Pengukuran:
Survey kepuasan dengan beberapa dimensi:
1. Kualitas Produk (bobot 30%)
2. Kualitas Layanan (bobot 30%)
3. Harga (bobot 20%)
4. Kemudahan Penggunaan (bobot 20%)

Skala: 1-5 (1=Sangat Tidak Puas, 5=Sangat Puas)

Formula:
CSI = (Total Score / Max Score) × 100%

Target:
- Q3 2025: 86%
- Q4 2025: 90%

==============================================

3. CUSTOMER RETENTION RATE

Definisi:
Persentase pelanggan yang tetap menggunakan layanan dalam periode tertentu.

Formula:
Retention Rate = ((E-N)/S) × 100%

Dimana:
- E = Jumlah customer di akhir periode
- N = Jumlah customer baru yang acquired selama periode
- S = Jumlah customer di awal periode

Target:
- Q3 2025: 90%
- Q4 2025: 93%

Interpretasi:
- >90%: Excellent
- 85-90%: Good
- 75-85%: Average
- <75%: Needs Improvement

==============================================

4. CHURN RATE

Definisi:
Persentase pelanggan yang berhenti menggunakan layanan dalam periode tertentu.

Formula:
Churn Rate = (Lost Customers / Total Customers at Start) × 100%

Relationship:
Churn Rate = 100% - Retention Rate

Target:
- Q3 2025: < 10%
- Q4 2025: < 7%

Acceptable Churn (by Industry):
- SaaS: 5-7% annual
- E-commerce: 20-30% annual
- Subscription Services: 10-15% annual

==============================================

5. REVENUE GROWTH

Definisi:
Persentase pertumbuhan pendapatan dibandingkan periode sebelumnya.

Formula:
Growth % = ((Current Revenue - Previous Revenue) / Previous Revenue) × 100%

Types:
- Month-over-Month (MoM) Growth
- Quarter-over-Quarter (QoQ) Growth
- Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth

Target:
- Monthly: 5-8%
- Quarterly: 15-20%
- Yearly: 50-60%

==============================================

6. EMPLOYEE SATISFACTION SCORE

Definisi:
Tingkat kepuasan karyawan terhadap lingkungan kerja, kompensasi, dan kultur perusahaan.

Cara Pengukuran:
Internal survey dengan aspek:
1. Work-Life Balance
2. Compensation & Benefits
3. Career Growth
4. Management Support
5. Company Culture

Skala: 0-100

Interpretasi:
- 80-100: Excellent
- 70-80: Good
- 60-70: Fair
- <60: Needs Attention

Target: 75-82

==============================================

7. CUSTOMER ACQUISITION COST (CAC)

Definisi:
Biaya rata-rata untuk mendapatkan satu pelanggan baru.

Formula:
CAC = Total Marketing & Sales Costs / Number of New Customers

Benchmark:
- Harus < 1/3 dari Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Payback period: idealnya < 12 bulan

==============================================

8. CUSTOMER LIFETIME VALUE (CLV)

Definisi:
Total revenue yang dihasilkan dari satu pelanggan selama masa berlangganan.

Formula:
CLV = (Average Purchase Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan)

Target Ratio:
CLV:CAC ratio > 3:1 (idealnya 5:1)

==============================================

FREQUENCY PENGUKURAN:

Daily Metrics:
- Revenue

Weekly Metrics:
- Churn rate

- NPS
